Addis Ababa University (AAU) has implemented a selective admissions policy to attract top-tier students across its diverse academic programs. The admission process is designed to be comprehensive, ensuring that candidates not only meet academic standards but also align with the university's values of inclusivity and diversity. ([aau.edu.et](https://www.aau.edu.et/blog/addis-ababa-universitys-selective-admissions-policy-to-take-effect-as-of-new-academic-year/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

**Undergraduate Admission Process**

1. **Eligibility Criteria**:
   - **Educational Background**: Applicants must have completed the Ethiopian Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination (ESSLCE) administered by the Ministry of Education and achieved passing marks as required by the Ministry for the relevant academic years.
   - **Entrance Examination**: Prospective students are required to take the Undergraduate Admission Test (UAT) conducted by AAU and obtain the minimum passing score. ([portal.aau.edu.et](https://portal.aau.edu.et/Web/Announcement/List?instructionId=97c0e789-1418-4f4d-8248-a70f4a03304d&studyLevel=Undergraduate&utm_source=chatgpt.com))

2. **Application Steps**:
   - **Create an Account**: Visit the AAU admission portal and create an applicant account using a valid email address and phone number. ([admission.aau.edu.et](https://admission.aau.edu.et/HowToApply?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
   - **Complete Academic Profile**: Log in to your account and fill out the academic profile section with accurate information.
   - **Select Program**: Choose your desired undergraduate program from the list of available options.
   - **Upload Documents**: Provide necessary documents, including your 12th-grade result certificate and transcripts for grades 9 to 12.
   - **Entrance Exam Registration**: Select your preferred location for the UAT and proceed to payment.
   - **Payment**: Pay the application and examination fees using the designated payment methods, such as Telebirr.
   - **Confirmation**: After payment, download your UAT admission ticket and check the exam schedule through the portal.

3. **Post-Examination**:
   - **Results**: Access your UAT results via the AAU portal.
   - **Admission Status**: If you meet the cut-off score, submit your educational credentials in person at the Registrar's Office located at the Sidist Kilo (Main Campus).

**Graduate Admission Process**

1. **Eligibility Criteria**:
   - **Educational Qualifications**: Applicants should hold a relevant undergraduate degree from a recognized institution.
   - **Graduate Admission Test (GAT)**: All candidates are required to take and pass the GAT administered by AAU. ([acewm-aau.org](https://acewm-aau.org/admission?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

2. **Application Steps**:
   - **GAT Registration**: Follow the instructions on the AAU website to register for the GAT.
   - **Online Application**: Upon passing the GAT, fill out the online application form via the AAU portal.
   - **Document Submission**: Upload necessary documents, including degree certificates, transcripts, and, for international applicants, an equivalence certificate from the Higher Education Relevance and Quality Agency (HERQA).
   - **Application Fee**: Pay the application fee through the specified payment channels.

3. **Selection Process**:
   - **Departmental Review**: Applications are forwarded to the respective departments for further screening based on specific criteria.
   - **Admission Decision**: Selected candidates receive an acceptance letter along with login credentials for course registration.

**Important Considerations**

- **Inclusivity and Diversity**: AAU's admissions policy emphasizes inclusiveness, providing opportunities for students with disabilities, females, and individuals from underrepresented communities. ([aau.edu.et](https://www.aau.edu.et/blog/addis-ababa-universitys-selective-admissions-policy-to-take-effect-as-of-new-academic-year/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Autonomous Admission**: As Ethiopia's first autonomous public university, AAU has the privilege of selecting its students, aiming to admit high-caliber individuals to enhance academic excellence.
- **Compliance**: The Office of the University Registrar ensures that all admissions processes adhere to relevant laws and university policies, maintaining the integrity and confidentiality of student records. ([aau.edu.et](https://www.aau.edu.et/admission?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
